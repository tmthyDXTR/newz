                  <p class="news-block">It was an upset-filled day one in the Romanian capital as <a href="/team/6248/the-mongolz" data-tooltip-id="uid1752579868">The MongolZ</a>, <a href="/team/5378/virtuspro" data-tooltip-id="uid536372688">Virtus.pro</a>, <a href="/team/11283/falcons" data-tooltip-id="uid386006502">Falcons</a>, <a href="/team/5973/liquid" data-tooltip-id="uid2128098165">Liquid</a> and <a href="/team/6667/faze" data-tooltip-id="uid610589272">FaZe</a> all stumbled at the first hurdle, sending them to the tournament's 0-1 pool.</p>
